Copy

Body copy is set in Roboto Regular. The base font size is set at 18px. All other sizes are relative. Text should stretch no further than 54 rem.

Admired for its natural beauty. Envied for its winter. Mocked for its quirks.

Florida is ephemeral to many except the nearly 19 million who call the Sunshine State home.

Sea level rise and other environmental change will drastically re-shape the unique ecosystem, communities and ways of life here in the next 100 years. Many are bearing witness to the first stage of this transformation and the loss which accompanies it.

Endangered Florida weaves the stories of the state's resillient citizens together into a single voice as they fight and adapt as nature follows its course.

This is text with emphasis and this has strong emphasis.
